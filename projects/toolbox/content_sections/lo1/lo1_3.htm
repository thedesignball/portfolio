Try it

This page contains activities about organising the cleaning storage area. Your trainer may decide to use the Discussion and Workplace activities as part of your assessment for the task. Check with your trainer before completing these activities.

Quiz

Complete this interactive quiz.

Discussion

In your group, discuss how you would organise your cleaning storage area. Consider the layout of the cleaning storage area and the positioning of items you would need. Take notes of your discussion - you will find them useful when completing your assessment for this task.

Workplace activity

If you already work in the cleaning industry, find a copy of an order form that is used by your company. Complete the order form and show it to your trainer.

If you are not working in the cleaning industry or cannot get a copy of an order form, use Squeak eClean's Order form (PDF 109KB). Complete it and show it to your trainer.
