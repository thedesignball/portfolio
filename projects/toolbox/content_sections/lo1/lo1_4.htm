Apply it

Several different types of assessments are listed on this page. Have a look at each type and meet with your trainer to decide on an assessment that is right for you. Your trainer may require you to complete more than one type of assessment for the task. Make sure that you discuss your preferred assessment option with your trainer.

Task assessment

Answer questions related to this task and give your answers to your trainer for marking.

  • Answer the questions in the Task assessment workbook (Word 63KB) and either print or send your answers as an email attachment to your trainer.
  • Answer the questions in an email and send to your trainer.

Verbal assessment

You may feel more comfortable discussing your answers with your trainer. If you decide to do this, use the questions in the Task assessment workbook (Word 63KB) to help you prepare. Organise a time to see your trainer.

Workplace assessment

This assessment is suitable for learners who are currently employed in the cleaning industry. You will need to answer questions related to your cleaning tasks in the workplace..

  • Answer the questions in the Workplace assessment workbook (Word 62KB) and either print or send your answers as an email attachment to your trainer.
  • Answer the questions in an email and send to your trainer.

Way forward >

You have reached the end of this task. Check with your trainer to see if you are required to complete any further activities or assessments.
